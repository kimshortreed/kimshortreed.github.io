Pulling the chute: options for tactical bailouts

You might reach a point when your body tells you that you have had enough attrition, whatever that means for you. Statistics Canada's data from 2018—the latest data available for this stat—shows that roughly 50% of doctoral degree students graduate after six years, which leaves roughly 50% of us either grinding onward or bailing.
